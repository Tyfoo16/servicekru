# Provider Interest Questionnaire

**Purpose:** Measure interest, motivation, and barriers among Malaysian women considering becoming certified home service professionals through a training & education platform (ServiceKru or similar).

**Estimated time to complete:** 4–5 minutes
**Target sample size:** Minimum 100 responses for directional insights; 300+ for segmentation
**Recommended channels:** WhatsApp, Facebook groups (Mama Bijak, Suri Rumah Berkerjaya), TikTok, community centres, PERKESO touchpoints
**Recommended incentive:** RM10 e-wallet credit OR guaranteed priority slot in next training intake

## Notes for the Research Team

**Data analysis priorities:**
1. **Funnel sizing** — % at "Very interested" (Q9 = 4 or 5) AND able to start within 3 months (Q18) AND has 10+ hours available (Q6). This is your addressable pipeline.
2. **Price sensitivity** — Cross-tab Q16 (willingness to pay) against Q5 (income) and Q3 (education) to set training tier pricing.
3. **Barrier segmentation** — Q12 results determine which trust signals to amplify in marketing (safety vs. cost vs. family approval).
4. **Channel ROI** — Q19 tells you where to spend acquisition budget for the next intake.

**Suggested follow-ups (depth interviews):**
Recruit 8–10 respondents who scored 4–5 on Q9 but flagged high barriers in Q12, for 30-min calls. This is where the real product insight lives.

**Translation:**
Build a Bahasa Malaysia version before launch. Recommend back-translation by a second translator to catch terminology drift (especially "certified", "professional", "training tier").
