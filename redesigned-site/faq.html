Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ServiceKru services, booking, and becoming a professional.

ServiceKru offers three main services:

  • Home Cleaning: Professional cleaning for homes, offices, and Airbnb units starting from RM120
  • Childcare Support: Flexible hourly care for children at home or centers
  • Elder Care Companion: Compassionate support for seniors including companionship and daily assistance from RM130 per session

Home cleaning services are standardized at RM120 per cleaning session. This includes deep cleaning of all rooms, kitchen and bathroom sanitization, and floor cleaning. The price is fixed to ensure transparency and fairness for both customers and our professional ServiceKrus.

Yes, all ServiceKru services are fully insured by [Provider]. Every booking is protected for your peace of mind, covering both the service professional and your property during the service.

Booking is simple:

  1. Choose your preferred service and fill out the booking form
  2. Select your date and time
  3. Provide your contact details and service address
  4. Click "Confirm Booking — WhatsApp" to send your booking request
  5. We'll match you with a certified professional and confirm via WhatsApp

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your booking. Please contact us via WhatsApp at +60 12 345 6789 at least 24 hours before your scheduled service time. We'll work with you to find a suitable alternative time or process your cancellation.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to ensure availability of our certified professionals. However, same-day bookings may be possible depending on demand and location. Contact us via WhatsApp to check availability.

Becoming a ServiceKru is a straightforward process:

  1. Apply online through our contact form
  2. Attend our comprehensive training program
  3. Get certified in your chosen service area
  4. Start receiving job assignments with flexible scheduling

All our professionals are women who receive proper training and certification.

ServiceKru professionals earn competitive rates:

  • Home Cleaning: RM120 per job
  • Childcare: RM80 per hour
  • Elder Care: RM130 per session

You can work flexibly and choose your schedule. Many professionals earn RM1,000–RM3,000 monthly depending on their availability and the number of jobs they accept.

No prior professional experience is required. We provide comprehensive training for all our ServiceKru professionals. What matters most is your commitment to providing quality service and your willingness to learn. Our training covers everything from basic service skills to safety protocols and customer service.

Yes, all ServiceKru professionals undergo thorough background checks before being certified. We ensure safety and reliability for every home and office through comprehensive verification processes, including reference checks and criminal background screening.

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with the service, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp. We'll work to resolve the issue, which may include sending another professional at no extra cost or providing a refund. We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Our referral program rewards you for sharing ServiceKru with friends and family:

  • Earn RM50 credit for each successful referral who books a service
  • No limit on referrals - earn as much as you want
  • Referrers get priority booking and special discounts
  • Contact us to start referring and track your rewards
